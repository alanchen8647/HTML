Unadat.com Landing Page

2024 Beta UX summer intern

In a team of seven, we adhered to the weekly Scrum cycle to redesign a new landing page for the start-up Unadat. Before diving into coding, we collaborated to create a series of product images using Microsoft Copilot and PS software. Concurrently, the sub-team I was in learned and utilized Figma to draft a blueprint for the new landing page. Throughout the redesign process, CEO Sean and IT supporter Dan offered valuable feedback on aspects like the frequency of call-to-action buttons and the impact of different section layouts on user attention.

After finalizing the blueprint, we divided the tasks among the team and began developing the new page on Google Cloud Shell. We primarily utilized HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap to build the new landing page. The initial coding phase took about a week, but the polishing phase required the most time. Sean provided extensive feedback on minor adjustments that could significantly enhance the user experience, such as the use of images and the arrangement of sections.


Challenges: Communicate and compromise different ideas, assisting other teammates who are not familiar with HTML and Bootstrap, and brainstorm possible user scenarios.
