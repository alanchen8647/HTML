Figma => EasyVerse, an Android AudioBook designed for people with disabilities

CreateSC 2024 UI/UX Designathon

Collaborated with a partner from the University of Central Florida to develop a mobile application platform aimed at enhancing accessibility and enriching the entertainment experience for individuals who need medical accommodations.

Our final product is a Mobile Audiobook application that aimed ADHD, visual impairment, and color blindness user groups. We aimed so increase the user's concentration by limiting the amount of information in each user interface inorder to reduce the distraction and increase the clarity of our application.
